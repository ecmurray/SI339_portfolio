About me

Hi, I’m Helena! I’m a creative and curious UX design and computer science student at the University of Michigan, and I’m passionate about designing engaging and imaginative experiences that are inclusive of all people.

With a heavy background in fine arts and programming, I have been bringing my experience as both a backend and frontend developer and designer to small businesses in Ann Arbor as a freelancer for over 2 years. My initiative and drive keep me constantly on the lookout for new opportunities to hone my skills and bring more to the table.

Beyond my studies, I am also a professional model with representation in Chicago, New York, and Los Angeles, which has allowed me to travel to so many new places and learn how to work with language barriers on ever-changing teams! In my free time I love to paint, I’m passionate about fashion and I like thrifting vintage clothes, and I spend a ton of time cooking new recipes and being outside in nature.
